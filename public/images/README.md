# Image Assets

Place your Pleasant Stay Motel photos here:

## Required Images

1. **DSC01938.jpg** - Main property exterior/landscape shot
   - This will be the hero image (first thing visitors see)
   - Recommended size: 1920x1080px or larger

2. **room1.jpg** - King Room
   - Recommended size: 800x600px
   - Show clean, well-lit room with king bed

3. **room2.jpg** - Queen Room
   - Recommended size: 800x600px
   - Show clean, well-lit room with queen bed

## Image Tips

- Use high-quality, well-lit photos
- Ensure rooms look clean and inviting
- Consistent lighting across all photos
- Optimize images before uploading (compress for web)
- JPG format works best for photos
